The books by Dr. Albert Grimes were under copyright by Dr. Grimes, who was a pastor with the Assembly of God for many years, and taught in Bible colleges in Canada. He started the Christian Training Center in 1966, having moved to Visalia, California. He passed away, and was promoted to glory on July 25, 1982, at the age of 73. His books are now freely distributed, and may be downloaded and shared without penalty. Enjoy.
